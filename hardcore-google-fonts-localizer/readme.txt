== Description ==

### Hardcore Google Fonts Localizer:

#### Adjustable in backend

* Define which Google Fonts you want to use (simply paste the URL, eg. https://fonts.googleapis.com/css?family=Montserrat:400,400i,700,700i|Open+Sans:400,400i,700,700i)
* Font including of following themes and Plugins can be disabled:
  - Jupiter by Artbees
  - WP Bakery Page Builder
  - Slider Revolution by Themepunch


== Installation ==

=== From within WordPress ===

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
1. Search for 'Hardcore Google Fonts Localizer'
1. Activate Hardcore Google Fonts Localizer from your Plugins page.
1. Go to "after activation" below.

=== Manually ===

1. Upload the `google-font-localizer` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the Hardcore Google Fonts Localizer plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to "after activation" below.

=== After activation ===

1. You should find the Hardcore Google Fonts Localizer Menu-item as a Submenu-item of Settings.
1. Paste the Google fonts inlcude URL into the text field and save the settings.
1. You're done!
